### Compile, Load, and Unload iio_dummy

Para alterar o `.config` precisei rodar o 
```
make -C "$IIO_TREE" menuconfig
# Or
make nconfig
```

sudo rmmod iio_dummy
sudo rm /lib/modules/$(uname -r)/kernel/drivers/iio/dummy/iio_dummy.ko
sudo depmod -a
